GENCG Working Journal - Introduction

Welcome to my Working Journal for the module Generative Computer Graphics. Over the course of three weeks we've been developing generatively created computergraphics. During this time we have been supervised and supported by Guillaume Massol. He's a teacher at the HSLU (Hochschule Luzern) and also works for the studio iart.
In this journal I documented my progress, including sketches on paper, code snippets, screenshots and interactive code demonstrations.
